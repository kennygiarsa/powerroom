What To Expect

THE EXPERIENCE

Every gathering is intentionally designed to create space for genuine encounter.

ELECTRIC WORSHIP

Our worship team brings an explosive, Spirit-led sound that breaks barriers. We don't just sing songs — we create an atmosphere where heaven meets earth.

RAW WORD

No sugarcoating. No religious jargon. Just the unfiltered truth of Scripture applied to the real issues young people face — mental health, identity, relationships, purpose.

POWER PRAYER

We believe in the power of prayer — real, bold, expectant prayer. Every gathering has dedicated time for personal ministry, healing, and encountering the Holy Spirit.

REAL COMMUNITY

Small groups are the heartbeat of Power Room. Weekly gatherings in homes across the city where you can be known, be challenged, and grow alongside people who actually care.

OUTPOURINGS

Monthly extended worship and prayer nights that go deeper. No clock. No agenda. Just hungry hearts pressing in for more of God. These nights have become legendary for breakthroughs.

GROWTH TRACK

Discipleship isn't optional — it's the way. Our Growth Track takes you from newcomer to leader through intentional mentoring, Bible study, serving, and leadership development.
